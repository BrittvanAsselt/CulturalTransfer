Academic Profile

My name is Britt van Asselt. I am a student of Early Medieval Insular Languages and Culture in the Research Master Ancient, Medieval, and Renaissance Studies at Utrecht University. This is also where I previously completed my BA (bachelor) in Celtic Languages and Culture. I am very interested in research on battles, rebellions, and invasions, as well as on the cultural and linguistic changes that can follow events like these. I am also interested in very different topics, particularly in the fields of genocide and religious studies. In my upcoming research I intend to focus on Scottish culture. This portal is a starting point for that.
