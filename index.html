Britt van Asselt

Academic Profile

Britt van Asselt is a student of Early Medieval Insular Languages and Culture in the Research Master Ancient, Medieval, and Renaissance Studies at Utrecht University, where previously she completed her BA in Celtic Languages and Culture. She specializes in research on battles, rebellions, and invasions, as well as on the cultural and linguistic changes such events produced. Additionally, she explores topics beyond her specialization, particularly in the fields of genocide and religious studies. In her upcoming research she intends to focus on Scottish culture.
